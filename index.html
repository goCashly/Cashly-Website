Frequently Asked Questions

Short answers to common borrower questions.

What is private lending?

Private lending is a way to borrow against your property through private lenders instead of a traditional bank.

What if I was rejected by a bank?

That is one of the most common reasons borrowers explore private mortgages. Cashly helps you understand whether it is the right fit.

Are there hidden fees?

The goal is the opposite: clear visibility into rates, fees, and tradeoffs before you move forward.

How quickly can I receive funds?

Timelines depend on the file, but private lending can often move faster than traditional bank approvals.
