About Cashly

How It All Started

Because "No" Shouldn’t Mean the End of the Road.

Cashly didn’t start in a boardroom; it started by watching good people get stuck. We saw homeowners with equity, dreams, and solid plans get turned away by big banks because they didn’t fit a specific box.

Worse, we saw what happened next: they were often met with a private lending world that felt like a black box, full of hidden fees, rushed decisions, and take-it-or-leave-it attitudes.

We realized that the problem wasn’t a lack of money in the market; it was a lack of clarity. We built Cashly to be the bridge, where private lending means strategic lending guided by transparency.

We’re here to make an impact

Our Mission

To replace confusion with clarity. We provide homeowners with the transparent data and expert guidance they need to navigate private mortgages safely and confidently.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted name in private lending by proving that honesty and empathy are the most powerful tools in finance.
