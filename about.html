About Cashly

We started off as a brokerage, trying to figure out the best workflow to close more deals while spending less time on mundane and tedious tasks. That’s why we built Cashly, an advanced CRM designed specifically for mortgage brokers. Our platform harnesses the power of AI to automate the entire mortgage process, from application to approval, allowing agents to focus on building relationships and closing more deals. Created by Mortgage Brokers, for Mortgage Brokers—transforming the way the industry operates.
