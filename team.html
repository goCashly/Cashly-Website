Pavit Paul

Founder & CEO

Pavit is the Founder and CEO of Cashly, an alternative lending platform helping freelancers and self-employed individuals get easy access to capital. Born and raised in India, Pavit, since his early childhood had the entrepreneurial spirit. Hailing from a business family, he learned the ways of business from a very young age. After completing high school, Pavit moved to Toronto to pursue his undergrad. He then started working, doing direct face-to-face sales for Fortune 500 companies. This gave him hands-on experience in sales strategy, marketing, and team building.
