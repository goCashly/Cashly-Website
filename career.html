Open role

Mortgage Agent

We are looking for a mortgage agent who can guide borrowers through private mortgage options with clarity, urgency, and a strong understanding of lender fit.

Toronto, Canada Flexible Full Time

What you will help with

  • Guide borrowers through purchase, refinance, and HELOC conversations with clearer expectations.
  • Review property details, timelines, equity position, and lender fit to help structure realistic next steps.
  • Work closely with the team to keep files moving cleanly from first call to funding path.

What we are looking for

  • Mortgage agent experience with strong comfort handling alternative or private lending conversations.
  • Clear communication, strong follow-up, and the ability to guide borrowers without pressure.
  • A practical understanding of timelines, lender expectations, and how to keep a file realistic.
