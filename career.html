Cashly is building a more thoughtful mortgage platform for borrowers, brokers, and teams that are tired of fragmented systems. We care about product craft, speed, and software that actually supports real work.

Why join

We are building software around real mortgage pressure, not generic templates.

Cashly started inside an operating mortgage business, so the problems are real: too much manual work, too much context switching, and not enough clarity for borrowers when decisions matter.

That means the people who join Cashly get to work on meaningful product problems across borrower guidance, lender matching, workflow automation, and the broker workspace behind the scenes.

Our environment

Fast-moving, collaborative, and close to the product.

We value people who care about clean execution, strong product instincts, and helping the team ship work that actually improves the mortgage experience.

What matters here

The kind of people who do well at Cashly.

Product judgment

We want people who can see the user problem clearly and make decisions that improve the real experience.

Comfort with ambiguity

Collaborative execution

Open role

Full Stack Software Engineer

We are looking for a hands-on engineer who cares about product quality, startup speed, and building software that helps mortgage teams operate more clearly.

Toronto, Canada Hybrid Full Time

What you will help build

  • Full stack product flows that support borrower guidance, broker workflows, and lender coordination.
  • Scalable front-end and back-end systems using modern web tooling.
  • Cleaner integrations, APIs, and workflow infrastructure that reduce manual work.

What we are looking for

  • 3+ years building full stack applications, ideally with React and Node.js.
  • Experience with REST APIs, integrations, Git, Docker, and fast iteration cycles.
  • Comfort working closely with product decisions in a startup environment.
